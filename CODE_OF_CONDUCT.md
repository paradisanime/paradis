## Our Pledge

We aim to keep this public release space respectful for users, contributors,
and maintainers discussing installation, bugs, and feature ideas.

## Our Standards

```
  Expected
  ........ Be clear and constructive
  ........ Focus on facts and reproduction details
  ........ Respect privacy (no dumping other users' data)

  Unacceptable
  ............ Harassment, hate, or personal attacks
  ............ Spam / promotional flooding
  ............ Sharing piracy invitations for unrelated warez markets
  ............ Publishing secrets, tokens, or private source dumps
```

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, lock, or remove content that violates this standard,
and may restrict participation for repeated abuse.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns related to this repository, open a private contact path
described by the maintainers (or use GitHub's reporting tools when needed).
Security vulnerabilities belong in SECURITY.md, not public arguing threads.
